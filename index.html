About Us

Our founder Miss Charmaine started this spa first known as Anneka body spa in the year 2019 as she needed a way to lose her belly fat. That is when she learnt about ultrasonic cavitation that melts away fat on targeted areas. She learnt more on it and then after it had worked for her she decided to introduce it into the market to assist those who were struggling with the same problem.

Our main aim is to assist our clients to get rid of their flaws. Miss Charmaine says "my belly fat always embarrassed me".
